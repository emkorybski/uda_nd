Short introduction

I have been designing and developing for the Web since 2006.

Working with Web technologies had been a passion that turned into a profession for me.

Over the years I have been engaged in a number of projects.

Some of those projects are no longer available online but you can still take a peek at them here.

My specialties

  1. Designing single-page sites that will work as a Web business card - first step for every company to mark their Web presence.
  2. Adding and optimizing device-related responsiveness to existing websites and applications (so that the sites display consistently across most mobile Web browsers).
  3. Developing custom lightweight blogs.
  4. Custom theming and enriching Tumblr blogs.
  5. Building custom rich media digital ads for online marketing campaigns.
